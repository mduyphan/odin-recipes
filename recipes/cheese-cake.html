Cheese Cake

Description

Cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert made from a mixture of soft cheese, eggs, and sugar, often set atop a crumbly crust and topped with fruit or sauces.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • 24 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 cup confectioners' sugar for dusting

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lighty grease a 9x13-inch pan.)
  2. Make yellow cake mix according to package directions. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Mix together the ricotta cheese, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract and spoon over cake batter.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175degrees C) for about 45 minutes. Sprinkle cake with confectioners' sugar when cool.
